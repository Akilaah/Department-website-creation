Classrooms and Lecture Halls: Spacious and well-equipped classrooms with audio-visual aids for effective teaching.

Laboratories: Specialized labs for different engineering disciplines, such as computer labs, electronics labs, mechanical labs, civil engineering labs, etc., where students can conduct experiments and gain hands-on experience.

Library: A well-stocked library with a vast collection of engineering books, journals, research papers, and digital resources.

Computer Center: A dedicated computer center equipped with the latest hardware and software for computer-related courses and projects.

Workshops: Workshops equipped with machinery and tools for students to work on practical projects and develop their technical skills.

Project Rooms: Dedicated spaces where students can collaborate and work on engineering projects.

Auditorium: An auditorium for conducting seminars, workshops, guest lectures, and other events.

Sports Facilities: Indoor and outdoor sports facilities to promote physical fitness and extracurricular activities.

Hostel and Accommodation: On-campus or nearby hostel facilities for students who come from other cities or countries.
